About Me

My name is Ninad AKA doffycosplay. I love watching anime, since I was a kid I used to watch anime. I was introduced to cosplay in 2016 at WCS finals India in Mumbai. I did my first cosplay in 2018. Since then I've done a total of 7 cosplays. It's been an interesting journey up until now being as a cosplayer. I always try to learn new stuff. The basic idea behind cosplaying is to recreate the characters and the most captivative part is how creative can you be? I always try to be creative in every aspect while cosplaying. Cosplaying has been soo much fun.
